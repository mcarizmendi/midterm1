The Paths to New York City

Escape from Russia


Although the majority of immigrants part of the shelter system in NYC are from South American countires. There are also a large amount from eastern countries.


This family is among more than 3,300 Russians who have passed through the city’s shelter system.


The family of 4 had to flee Russia when President Vladimir V. Putin began to crack down on dissent against the war in Ukraine. In search of proper medical care for Ms. Antipkina’s autoimmune disease, they ended up in New York in May 2023. And have been lucky enough to stay in the Roosevelt hotel for over a year.


Not everyone has it that easy. Families are allowed to stay in shelters for up to 60 days before having to move out and reapply. Often times leaving families left with an unknown feeling of where they will be sleeping next.
