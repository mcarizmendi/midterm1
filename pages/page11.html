The Paths to New York City

Here to work


Often times there is a miscoception about immigrants arriving to the U.S. Some people say that they come here expecting hand outs. That is not the case for many hard-working latino migrants.


Without knowing the hard long journey they face to even make it into the U.S. One couldn't not believe how ready they are to work and move foward in their lives.

This is the case for the Henao Family.


Not even a second week in New York and the young parents from Colombia were already diligently applying for work permits and jobs.
